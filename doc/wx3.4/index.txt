wiki2xtml is a '''[[w:Web_content_management_system client-side CMS]].''' Pages are written in Wikitext known from the Wikipedia and will then be converted into a webpage by wiki2xhtml.

If required, own code (like XHTML, PHP, JavaScript) can be used inside the pages, like for contact forms, Youtube videos, or animations.

Have fun!
